This step is optional; it further refines the population-level pathway correspondance through the elastic registration.
which can slightly improve the heritability or reliability.

step1: step31_pre
will randomly sample 300 subjects to generate the mean fiber FA profile curve.
step2: step32_parallel_KarcherMean
will generate the Karcher Mean FA curve for each of the 6090 pathways based on 300 randomly selected subject FA profiles.
step3: step33_parallel_reg_phase12_Lambda or step33_parallel_reg_phase4_Lambda.m
will register individual subject FA profiles to the Karcher mean for 6090 pathways, with regularization parameters selected according to reliability or heritability (default).
